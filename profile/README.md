# Oreon HQ
This org is home for Oreon, a Linux distribution maintained under Oreon HQ and the community. Oreon is aimed at people who want a stable long-term Linux OS that can last years without feeling outdated.

Current Oreon releases are based on AlmaLinux, but we are actively making progress towards being a fully independent RPM-based Linux distribution, planned for our next major release.
